# AI Model Comparison: Gemini vs OpenAI vs Anthropic

## Overview

This is a short comparison of Gemini, OpenAI, and Anthropic based on their core strengths and use cases.

---

## Gemini (Google)

Gemini is a multimodal model that can handle text and images. It is optimized for speed and low-cost usage, especially with Gemini Flash. It works well for real-time and large-scale processing tasks.

**Best for:** fast responses, image + text tasks, bulk processing.

---

## OpenAI (GPT models)

OpenAI models are strong in reasoning, coding, and tool/function calling. They are widely used for building AI applications and developer tools due to their reliability and structured output support.

**Best for:** coding, APIs, function calling, complex reasoning.

---

## Anthropic (Claude)

Claude models focus on clear reasoning, writing quality, and long-context understanding. They are effective for explanations, documentation, and analysis tasks.

**Best for:** writing, long-form content, summarization, analysis.

---

## Conclusion

* Gemini = speed + multimodal
* OpenAI = coding + tools + intelligence
* Anthropic = writing + reasoning quality

Most real systems combine all three depending on the task.
